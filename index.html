About

Bruce Rintoul is a record producer and mixer from Glasgow with over 7 years of experience

Early on in his career, he worked with high calibre bands such as Twin Atlantic and We Were Promised Jetpacks and from there his production style developed into what it is today. With a passion for arrangements and energy, Bruce has built his reputation by bringing out the best in his clients and, more importantly, the best in their songs.

From cutting his teeth in LO-FI studios in the heart of Glasgow, Bruce has progressed to work in a variety of Studios across the UK including 45 A-Side, Gorbals Sound, Rocco Studios and Rockfield . Alongside this, he has also collaborated with many renowned producers and mixers among which are Jason Perry, Adam Noble, Vince Ratti, Gil Norton and Will Yip.
